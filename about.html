About US-TAX.US

Our Mission

US-TAX.US was created to provide Americans with a simple, free tool to calculate sales tax across all 50 states. We believe financial tools should be accessible to everyone.

What We Do

Our calculator uses current tax rates to provide accurate estimates for any purchase in the United States. We update our data regularly to ensure accuracy.

Free & Accessible

We are committed to keeping this tool 100% free. There are no hidden fees, no registration required, and no personal data collection.

Disclaimer

US-TAX.US provides estimates for informational purposes only. We are not tax professionals and recommend consulting with a tax advisor for official tax calculations.
