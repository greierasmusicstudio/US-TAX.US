Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2024

1. Information We Collect

US-TAX.US is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect personal information when you use our tax calculator. All calculations are performed locally in your browser.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: To understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics may collect anonymous data about your visit.
  • Google AdSense: To display advertisements. AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

3. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Google Analytics to track website traffic
  • Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements
  • Improving user experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Data Storage

We do not store any personal data from calculator usage. Any preferences (like recently used states) are stored locally in your browser.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: admin@us-tax.us
